What is Red Dust Festival?

Red Dust is a music festival held on the site of the former Consett Iron Company steel works in Consett, Co. Durham.
It takes place over August bank holiday weekend with Red Dust Rave on Saturday 26th and Red Dust Rock on Sunday 27th.
Set in the beautiful Derwent Valley, Red Dust Festival pays homage to the area's natural beauty and rich industrial heritage, celebrating the magic of live music and what makes Consett canny!

Red Dust Rave

Red Dust Rave is a 24h dance party with EDM, both old and new from a wide range of genres including: House, Techno, Drum & Bass, Garage, Bassline and Dubstep. The party doesn't stop in the Red Dust Rising tent with the Red Dust Ravers spinning original mixes and 90s throwbacks at the silent rave from 12am-4am.

Red Dust Rock

Red Dust Rock celebrates the best of Britpop, UK indie and alternative rock. Expect sing-along anthems from Kaiser Chiefs, Stereophonics, The Vaccines and more! Join us in the Red Dust Rising tent for the best new music from up-and-coming artists in 2023.

Transport & Parking

Car

Field parking is available at Red Dust. Please follow the yellow road signs on your approach to the site.

The colour of your permit determines the area you should park:

  • Orange - Standard Parking
  • Blue - Accessible Parking
  • Red - Caravans & Motorhomes
  • Green - Staff Parking

Please display your parking permit in your car's window to verify your vehicle with the stewards on arrival.
